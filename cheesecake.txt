Preheat oven 100c
melt
     120g cream cheese
     80g milk
     35g unsalted butter
     flavors
add
     35g All purpose flour
     40g egg yolks
beat, must cold
soft peaks
     80g egg whites 
     1ml acid
     40g sugar 

tray with hot water around 2cm height and bake

100c/214f for 60
160c/320f for 15 or until brown
